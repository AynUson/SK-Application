Anti-Rabies Control Program

Venue: New Kalalake, O.C

Date: March 16 28, 2021

Officer in Charge: Daryl John Tadeo

Description: The Barangay of New Kalalake has set its eyes in making the city rabies-free for the welfare and benefit of its constituents. In line with this goal, the City Veterinary Office of Olongapo City has intensified its campaign against rabies, particularly in the month of March which is Rabies Awareness Month, by holding mass anti-rabies vaccination activities and going to different barangays to conduct seminars on responsible pet ownership.

 Free Zumba Classes

Venue: Banicain, O.C

Date: September 15 2021

Officer in Charge: Geraldyne Ambrocio

Description: Residents of Banicain, Olongapo City need not go to gyms to be fit. Mayor Johncel Anjoe Gonzaga is sponsoring free zumba sessions in barangays. Interested parties should organize at least 20 individuals to attend the zumba sessions. Afterwards, they should submit a request letter to the mayor’s office stating the date and time when they would like the zumba classes to be held.

 Basketball League

Venue: Gordon Heights, O.C.

Date: April 13 2021

Officer in Charge: Ryunosuke Fujii

Description: Summer is the time for sports. The Federation of the Sangguniang Kabataan of Gordon Heights, Olongapo City spearheaded by Federated Sangguniang Kabataan President Ryu Fujii held a Summer Basketball League 2021 for youths ages 15 to 20 years old. The theme is “Values Development through Sports”. Almost every year the Sangguniang Kabataan will hold a basketball league for youths. The 19 Barangays of the municipality participates the event.

 Blood Drive Donation

Venue: New Cabalan, O.C.

Date: September 10 2019

Officer in Charge: Juan Dela Cruz

Description: Barangay of New Cabalan establish its own blood donor’s program in line with efforts to promote and encourage voluntary blood donation. Municipal Ordinance No. 302, s. 2018 aims to instill public consciousness that blood donation is a humanitarian act; provide adequate, safe and affordable supply of blood in every barangay promoting and encouraging voluntary blood donation; and inform the public of the importance of voluntary blood donation to curb hazards caused by commercial sale of blood.
